Non-Clinical Psychoeducational Curriculum

Structured trauma education with flow, language, and a clear path forward.

Capitol Contracts LLC develops the What Really Happened (WRH) project system: non-clinical education that teaches survival patterns, addiction cycles, freeze responses, shame loops, and nervous-system adaptation as understandable mechanisms — not character failures.

The system connects a lived-experience memoir, public counter-narrative articles, a 5-week introductory track, a 30-session pilot package, and a full 75-session master curriculum for institutional review and implementation planning.

What this is

WRH is a public-facing system built to make hard patterns visible. The memoir provides the source story. The articles translate the language. The core track introduces the framework. The pilot and master curriculum make it usable for programs, peers, and institutional partners.

Who it helps

The system is designed for organizations that need clear trauma education without requiring therapy participation, trauma disclosure, or diagnostic framing.

Recovery & Reentry Programs

Plain-language education for shame, avoidance, relapse patterns, shutdown, accountability, and survival behavior.

Veteran-Support Organizations

Mechanism-first language for guarded audiences who may resist therapy-style framing.

Peer & Community Programs

Facilitator-ready tools for structured discussion, pattern recognition, and non-clinical support.
