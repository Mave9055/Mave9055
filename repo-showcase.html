How the System Fits Together

The work is organized as a pipeline: story, language, curriculum, pilot, and implementation.

1. MemoirThe lived story: From the Storm to the Fire.
2. ArticlesPublic counter-narratives that translate survival patterns.
3. CoreTop 10 Core introduces the WRH language in five weeks.
4. PilotThe 30-session deployment package tests fit and implementation.
5. MasterThe 75-session curriculum provides the full architecture.
Next Step: Review the WRH Master Curriculum for the full 75-session structure, the Pilot Deployment Package for implementation planning, or the Capitol Contracts repository for company-facing documentation.
Scope Boundary: WRH materials are non-clinical psychoeducational resources. They are not therapy, medical care, diagnosis, crisis care, legal advice, or a substitute for licensed professional support. The purpose is language-building, pattern recognition, facilitator support, and structured education.
