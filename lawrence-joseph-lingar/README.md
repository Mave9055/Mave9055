# Lawrence Joseph Lingar Memorial Archive

A small public archive page for **Private Lawrence Joseph Lingar** of Lamar, Arkansas.

This folder preserves the key details from a wartime newspaper clipping showing Private Lingar in uniform and reporting that he was killed in action.

## Source note

The uploaded clipping filename indicates: `Times_Record_1943_02_28_16 (1).jpg`.

## Transcription from the clipping

> **KILLED IN ACTION** — Private Lawrence Joseph Lingar, son of Mr. and Mrs. Hugh Lingar, Lamar, Ark., was killed Jan. 10 in action in the Southwest Pacific area, according to a message received by his parents from the war department. Private Lingar was inducted into the army Feb. 16, 1942. He received his basic training in California. He attended Hartman high school, and was a member of the Ozone CCC camp at Clarksville.

## Key details captured

- **Name:** Lawrence Joseph Lingar
- **Rank:** Private
- **Parents:** Mr. and Mrs. Hugh Lingar
- **Home:** Lamar, Arkansas
- **Killed in action:** Jan. 10, likely 1943 based on the clipping filename/date
- **Theater:** Southwest Pacific area
- **Inducted into Army:** Feb. 16, 1942
- **Basic training:** California
- **School:** Hartman High School
- **CCC camp:** Ozone CCC camp at Clarksville
