Transcription

KILLED IN ACTION — Private Lawrence Joseph Lingar, son of Mr. and Mrs. Hugh Lingar, Lamar, Ark., was killed Jan. 10 in action in the Southwest Pacific area, according to a message received by his parents from the war department. Private Lingar was inducted into the army Feb. 16, 1942. He received his basic training in California. He attended Hartman high school, and was a member of the Ozone CCC camp at Clarksville.
